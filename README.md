Digital Construction is a web-based platform designed to solve real-world problems in the construction industry by bringing **verified builders, authentic materials, inspectors, and customers** together under one digital system.

The platform helps users plan, manage, and execute construction projects with transparency, verified documentation, real-time updates, and secure communication.

## ğŸš€ Project Overview

Building a house or managing a construction project is often stressful, time-consuming, and risky due to:
- Unverified builders
- Fake documentation
- Inconsistent material prices
- Lack of market transparency
- Difficulty finding skilled labor and inspectors

**Digital Construction** fixes this by offering a trusted, all-in-one construction management solution.
